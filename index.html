Our Story

Who We Are

J&J Remodeling Team LLC is a locally owned remodeling company based in Nashville, Tennessee. With five years of hands-on experience, we specialize in providing high-quality renovation and improvement services for residential properties.

Founded and operated by Lesther Gutierrez, our company is built on hard work, reliability, and attention to detail. We take pride in delivering results that not only meet expectations but exceed them.

Every project is handled with professionalism, precision, and a strong commitment to customer satisfaction. Our focus is simple: provide durable, functional, and visually appealing improvements that increase the value and comfort of your home.

Our Mission

Provide dependable and high-quality remodeling services that transform spaces and improve the daily lives of our clients.

Our Vision

Become a trusted remodeling company in Nashville and surrounding areas, recognized for quality work, professionalism, and long-term customer relationships.
